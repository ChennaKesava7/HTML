TCS

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company, headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India and largest TCS campus located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. As of February 2021, TCS is the largest IT services company in the world by market capitalisation ($169.2 billion). It is a subsidiary of the Tata Group and operates in 149 locations across 46 countries.
